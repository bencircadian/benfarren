Engram

The Problem

Students forget what they've learned. Teachers waste time on scattered planning. The forgetting curve wins, and lessons get repeated instead of building on solid foundations.

The Solution

For students: Spaced repetition with AI-generated questions that adapt to each learner. For staff: DNA (Do Now Activity) builders and planning resources to streamline lesson prep.

Where teaching meets

Technology

01
